How it started

The website Magical Chords started as a simple portfolio project. At the beginning it was only supposed to show chords after formatted (from a Chord Pro format), but it became more. From one single page, it became three, then ten, then a chord library! And now it's a place for the community. It's a place for people to help each other and express themselves.

What we aim

As music is something that makes the world more colorfoul and brings people together, our purpose couldn't be different. We want to have in our library as much song sheets as possible and as many instrument versions as well. After all, guitarists are not the only ones doing music out there! Besides that, we want to give everyone voice and let people decide what's best (read more in the website future improvements section).Furthermore, the intuitive and elegant design is a priority just as much as the powerful code behind it. We are always trying to improve the website to work better and to have the best usability as possible.

What can you do?

This is a website for the community. For the community to decide what's best, to help each other, to contribute for the benefit of all. We are not capable of putting every single song chords in the world here. We are not capable of checking if everything is correct. We are just a few humans. Sometimes we will fail and sometimes we won't be enough. Therefore, if you have any interest, in the future you will be able to upload chords, vote for versions, denounce offenses, and hopefully much more. Feedback is always welcome and extremely helpful. Thank you for making this possible and better.

Website future improvements

We intend to add the following features to the website, as soon as possible: denounce buttons, personal profiles, possibility to upload chords, votings for different chord versions, key changes, comments section, and much more.
